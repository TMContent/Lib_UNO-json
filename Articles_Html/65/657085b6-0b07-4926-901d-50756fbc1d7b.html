Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.6.1

What to Check For

The key management procedures should require strong encryption keys.

How to Check

Check the information security policy regarding key management procedures to make sure they require strong encryption keys.

How to Fix

Include a requirement for strong encryption keys in the key management procedures. A statement about the desired strength is appropriate.
