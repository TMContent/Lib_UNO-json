What to Do

Manage service providers that handle shared cardholder data.

Why

Service providers that handle shared cardholder data have to share responsibility for the security of that data.

How

If the company shares cardholder data with service providers for purposes such as back-ups or analysis, take the following actions:

  1. List providers. Make a list of all service providers who share cardholder data. Attach the list to the information security documentation.

  2. Make sure service providers acknowledge responsibility for security. Have each service provider who shares cardholder data sign a form, which states that the provider takes responsibility for securing cardholder data.

  3. Perform due diligence. Perform due diligence before engaging any service provider. Document the due diligence process and attach this information to the information security documentation.

  4. Monitor PCI DSS compliance of service providers. Check the PCI DSS compliance status of each service provider and attach this information to the information security policy. Delegate responsibility for monitoring PCI DSS compliance of service providers to a qualified staff member.
