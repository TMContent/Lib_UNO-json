Welcome to Team Academy. The following areas are available:

  • Standards - Information security guidelines have been combined together to form standards. Enter the Standards area to study them.
  • Programming Best Practices - Following a few programming best practices can effectively prevent most dangerous vulnerabilities from ever affecting your applications. Enter the Programming Best Practices area to find out about secure coding practices that are relevant to your projects.
  • Platform-specific Guidance - Different platforms implement security controls differently. Enter the Platform-specific Guidance area to find the most effective countermeasures for specific platforms.
  • Vulnerabilities - This area presents our extensive collection of vulnerability information, including information about how to prevent and remediate each type of vulnerability.
