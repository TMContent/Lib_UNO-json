Applies To

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.2.3

Policy Statement

Do not store PINs.

Policy Purpose

The cardholder PIN is used for authentication at ATMs. This makes it of high value to an attacker. In the rare event it is required for a transaction; it should not be stored after the transaction has been processed.

Related Guidance

  • Do Not Store Particularly Sensitive Cardholder Data
