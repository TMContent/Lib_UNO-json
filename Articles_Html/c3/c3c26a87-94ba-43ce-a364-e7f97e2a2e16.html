Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.1.6
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.1.6.a
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.1.6.b

What to Do

Review the firewall configuration at least every six months. Document the reviews. Add a point to the information security policy to require periodic firewall configuration reviews.

Why

Over time, the network configuration and business requirements might change. A regular review is required to make sure that the firewall configuration accurately matches the business needs while providing an adequate level of security.

How

Add a point to the information security policy to perform a review of the firewall configuration every six months. Designate staff to perform the review of the firewall configuration. Add the results of each review to the network documentation.
