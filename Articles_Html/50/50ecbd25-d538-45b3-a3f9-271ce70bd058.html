Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.6.a

What to Check For

The information security policy should include key-management procedures.

Why

Proper key management procedures ensure that cryptography is applied correctly and securely.

How to Check

Review the information security policy to make sure it includes key-management procedures.

How to Fix

Identify the company's cryptography needs. Design and implement key management procedures to accommodate those needs.
