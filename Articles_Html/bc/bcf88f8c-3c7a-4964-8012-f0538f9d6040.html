What to Check For

Check to ensure that Strong passwords are used for router administrative access.

How to Check

Brute force password software can launch more than just dictionary attacks. It can discover common passwords where a letter is replaced by a number. For example, if&nbsp;"p4ssw0rd" is used as a password, it can be cracked. Always use uppercase and lowercase, number, and symbol combinations when creating passwords.
