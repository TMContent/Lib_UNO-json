Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 11.1

What to Check For

A wireless IDS/IPS should be deployed or a wireless analyzer used at least once every quarter to detect unauthorized devices. The wireless IDS/IPS solution should identify all wireless devices in use and generate alerts when unauthorized devices or attacks are detected.

Why

A wireless IDS or IPS can detect unauthorized wireless devices. Detecting such devices clearly indicate the presence of an attack.

How to Check

Interview the responsible personnel to identify the location of the IDS/IPS and ask them to demonstrate the administration interface. Verify that the IDS/IPS is set up to notify staff if an unauthorized wireless device is detected.

How to Fix

Choose a wireless IDS or IPS solution. Configure the wireless IDS or IPS by following the vendor's instructions. Set the IDS or IPS to alert the staff in the event of a suspected compromise.
