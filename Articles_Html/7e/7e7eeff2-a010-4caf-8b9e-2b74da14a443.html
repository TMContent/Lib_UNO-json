Applies To

  • PCI DSS Requirement 8.7

Policy Statement

Protect databases from unauthorized access.

Policy Purpose

Preventing the database access accounts used by applications from being used interactively or by other processes ensures that if these accounts are compromised, an attacker cannot use them. Database access accounts should use authentication to prevent unauthorized access and to provide more detailed logs.
