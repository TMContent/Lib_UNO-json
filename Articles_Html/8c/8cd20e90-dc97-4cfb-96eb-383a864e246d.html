Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.6.3

What to Check For

The key management procedures should require secure storage.

How to Check

Check the information security policy to identify the key management procedures. Make sure that they include a provision for secure storage. Inspect the key management procedures to verify that they provide secure storage.

How to Fix

Design and document key management procedures to require secure storage. Implement the key management procedures.
