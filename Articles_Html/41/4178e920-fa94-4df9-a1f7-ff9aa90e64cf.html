Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.3
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.3.1
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.3.2

What to Do

Designate at least one DMZ. A DMZ is a network segment that contains machines that may handle incoming Internet traffic. There may be more than one DMZ.

Why

The DMZ is a defense-in-depth measure used to separate systems that are open to attacks from those that hold sensitive information. When a DMZ is implemented, even if the systems in the DMZ are compromised, the attacker does not have full access to the sensitive information.

How

Designate a network segment as the DMZ. Install and configure a firewall between the DMZ and the Internet. Install and configure a firewall between the DMZ and the internal network segment.
