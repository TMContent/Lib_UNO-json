Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 11.1

What to Do

Deploy a wireless IDS.

How

Choose and purchase a wireless IDS. When choosing a wireless IDS, check if it is possible to integrate the IDS with your centralized log server and to generate alerts. Deploy and configure the chosen wireless IDS by following the vendor's instructions.
