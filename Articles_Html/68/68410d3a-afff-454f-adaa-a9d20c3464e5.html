Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 12.7

What to Do

Conduct background checks on new employees.

Why

A background check assures that a potential new employee is not a known criminal and is representing himself truthfully.

When

Conduct background checks on new employees before hiring.

How

Contact&nbsp;an agency that can perform background checks. Attorneys and private investigators can often provide this service for a small fee.&nbsp;Instruct Human Resources staff to conduct background checks before hiring new employees who will have access to cardholder data or the cardholder data environment.
