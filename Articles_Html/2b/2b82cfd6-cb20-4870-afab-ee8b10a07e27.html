What to Do

Assign information security responsibilities.

How

Designate an information security team. Assign responsibility for information security to the Chief Information Security Officer or another qualified member of management. Assign the following responsibilities to formal staff positions in the company or to contractors:

  • Managing policies and procedures
  • Monitoring alerts, reviewing&nbsp;logs, and notifying the appropriate staff
  • Managing incident response procedures
  • Managing user accounts
  • Managing data access control
