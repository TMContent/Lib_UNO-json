Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.6.5.b

What to Do

Establish a procedure for replacing known or suspected compromised encryption keys.

Why

Compromised encryption keys may be used to decrypt sensitive information.

How

Design a procedure for changing the encryption keys. Document the procedure for changing the keys. Include the requirement and the procedure in the information security policy. Change any known or suspected compromised keys. You may have to add this functionality to your software if you don't have it already.
