Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 9.6

What to Do

Protect physical storage media in the cardholder data environment.

Why

Information storage media, which contains cardholder data, should be physically secured to ensure that a thief cannot steal it. This applies to paper hard copies as well as to digital media.

How

Different companies opt for different security measures. Generally speaking, physical security involves security guards, surveillance operators, surveillance equipment, physical barriers, alarms, locks, and card-based access controls. If your company has lax physical security, consider adding layers of the above-mentioned security measures until physical security is considered satisfactory.
