Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 5.2
  • PCI DSS Requirement 5.2.a

What to Check For

The information security policy should require updating of anti-virus software and definitions.

Why

The anti-virus definitions and anti-virus engine have to be updated frequently to be effective because malware threats evolve rapidly.

How to Check

Review the information security policy to make sure that it requires updating of anti-virus software and definitions. Review the anti-virus configuration to make sure it matches the policy requirements.

How to Fix

Add a point to the information security policy that requires updating of anti-virus software and definitions. Install and configure an anti-virus. Configure the anti-virus to update its engine and definitions. Designate a staff member to be responsible for maintaining the anti-virus system.
