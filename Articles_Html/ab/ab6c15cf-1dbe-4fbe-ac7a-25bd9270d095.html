Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.2.1.a
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.2.1.b
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.2.1.c

What to Do

Configure the firewalls to reject all network connections that are not required for business purposes. Use explicit "deny all" or implicit "deny after allow" settings to block unauthorized traffic by default.

Why

Allowing unauthorized connections may allow an attacker to conduct an attack and extract sensitive information.

How
