Applies to

Web Application

    What to Do

    Encrypt cookies and check them for integrity to detect tampering, even over an SSL channel.

    Why

    Encrypting cookies and checking for integrity ensures that cookie contents are not read and not tampered with.&nbsp; Cookies can be stolen, even over a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) channel using cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks.

    When

    Apply this guideline for all applications that use Forms Authentication.

    How

    Encrypt the cookie contents even if you are using SSL. This prevents an attacker viewing or modifying the cookie if he manages to steal it through an XSS attack. In this event, the attacker could still use the cookie to access your application, but only while the cookie remains valid.
