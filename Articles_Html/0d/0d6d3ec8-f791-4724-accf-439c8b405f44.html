Applies to

  • Microsoft® Windows Communication Foundation (WCF) 3.5
  • Microsoft Visual Studio® 2008

What to Do

Consider exposing multiple endpoints to support different authentication schemes, different protocols or to support ASMX clients.

Why

By exposing multiple endpoints you will get more flexibility.

When

When you want to support different authentication schemes, different protocols or to support ASMX clients.

How

You only have to develop the service once, while still providing clients with the endpoint that matches their security requirements.
