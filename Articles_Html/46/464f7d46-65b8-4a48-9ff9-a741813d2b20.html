Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 6.4

What to Check For

Change control documentation should include a management sign-off by responsible parties for each change.

Why

A management sign-off requirement ensures that only authorized changes are applied.

How to Check

Review the change control documentation to make sure it includes a management sign-off by responsible parties for each documented change.

How to Fix

Have the management sign-off on the documentation for the proposed changes before implementing them in the future.
