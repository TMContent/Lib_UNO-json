Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.6.4

What to Check For

The key management procedures should require the keys to be changed at least annually.

Why

Changing encryption keys periodically makes it more difficult for an attacker to get access to a valid key.

How to Check

Check the key management procedures in the information security policy to make sure they require the encryption keys to be changed at least annually.

How to Fix

Add a requirement to change the encryption at least annually to the information security policy as a part of the key management procedures. Change the encryption keys at least once a year.
