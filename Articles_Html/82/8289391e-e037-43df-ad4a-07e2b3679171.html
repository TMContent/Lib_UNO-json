Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.4.a
  • PCI DSS Requirement 1.4.b

What to Do

Install personal firewalls on mobile and/or employee-owned computers used to access the company network. Configure mobile and/or employee-owned computers used to access the company network to company standards.

Why

A compromised personal computer is a threat to the cardholder data environment. A personal firewall offers some protection from network attacks and may prevent remote control malware from working.

How

Install and configure a personal firewall on each mobile and/or employee-owned computer used to access the company network. On recent Windows operating systems, the built-in firewall may be used to meet this requirement. Use a group policy to prevent the employee from disabling the firewall or changing the firewall settings.
