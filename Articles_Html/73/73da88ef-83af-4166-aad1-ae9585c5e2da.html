Applies To

  • ASP.NET 4.0

What to Check For

Verify that custom error pages are not used to denote error states.

Why

Exceptions contain debugging information that could constitute an information disclosure. Be careful with custom error pages they may also implicitly provide unintended information as a side effect.

How To Check

Examine error pages to make sure that they are standardized and don't disclose debug information.

How To Fix

Standardize the error pages, and scrutinize their design and presentation carefully to avoid inadvertent information disclosure. Avoid displaying debug information in error pages.
