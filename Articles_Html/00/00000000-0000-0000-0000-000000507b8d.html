What to Do

Use the IISCrypto tool to configure the TLS cypher suites used by IIS.

Why

Choose strong TLS suites to avoid known vulnerabilities in TLS. Weak crypto suites are subject to crypto attacks which will decipher and compromise confidential data.

How

Use the free IISCrypto tool to choose strong TLS cypher suites:

  1. Download IISCrypto. IISCrypto may be downloaded at the link at the bottom of this article.
  2. Run IISCrypto. IISCrypto is easy to deploy and has a very simple interface. Download and run IISCrypto.
  3. Chooose your desired compliance standard(s). IISCrypto comes with presets based on common information security standards. Choose the ones that are the most applicable to your situation. In a couple of clicks, your IIS TLS cypher suites should be configured in compliance with the selected standards.
