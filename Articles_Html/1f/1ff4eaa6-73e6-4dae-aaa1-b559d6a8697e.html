Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.3

What to Check For

Some symbol should be used to "mask" all the digits of the PAN except for the last four on any page where the PAN is displayed and the viewer does not have a legitimate business need to see the entire PAN.

Why

PANs are very sensitive and displaying them on the screen or on print-outs is unacceptable.

How to Check

Make a list of all the pages where a PAN is displayed. View every source and the corresponding output page to make sure that the PAN is masked.

How to Fix

Implement masking in the function(s) used to print the PAN by substituting some character for all the digits in the PAN except the last four.
