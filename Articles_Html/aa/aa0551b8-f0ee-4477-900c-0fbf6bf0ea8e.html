Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 6.4

What to Check For

Ensure that all changes to software, including patches, are documented. Check that change-control documentation includes customer impact considerations, management authorization sign-offs, results of functionality testing, and back-out procedures.

Why

Changes should be documented to help find the cause of a vulnerability in case one is introduced by the changes.

How to Check

Check that change-control documentation exists and includes the following for each documented change: customer impact considerations, management authorization sign-offs, results of functionality testing, and back-out procedures.

How to Fix

Designate a testing environment. Prepare a folder for change control documentation. Test and document all changes, including patches, before deploying them. When documenting changes, include customer impact considerations, management authorization sign-offs, results of functionality testing, and back-out procedures.
