Applies To

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.2.1

Policy Statement

Do not store full track contents.

Policy Purpose

The full card track contents should not be stored to limit the potential damage in the event of a successful attack. The term "track contents" refers to the information stored on the magnetic strip of the physical card issued to a cardholder. New cards also include a chip, the contents of which should also be considered "track contents." If an attacker has this information, he may be able to use it to make fraudulent transactions; therefore, the full track contents should never be stored.
