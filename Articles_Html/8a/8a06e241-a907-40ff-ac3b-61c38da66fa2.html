Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4
  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4.a
  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4.b
  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4.c
  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4.d

What to Do

Render the PAN unreadable whenever it is stored.

Why

The PAN should be rendered unreadable whenever it is stored to prevent disclosure in the event of a successful compromise.

How

Identify if and where the PAN is stored. Choose one of the following mechanisms to protect the PAN: one-way crypto-hashes, truncation, index tokens and pads, and strong cryptography. Implement the chosen mechanism.
