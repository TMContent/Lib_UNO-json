Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.1

What to Do

Explicitly define business requirements for cardholder data retention. The requirements should be explicit regarding the duration of retention and the purpose. For example, “Cardholder data X should be stored for duration Y for the purpose Z.”

How

Identify the cardholder data retention requirements for legal, regulatory and business needs. Express the retention requirements in the form of "Cardholder data X should be stored for duration Y for the purpose Z," and include them in the information security policy.
