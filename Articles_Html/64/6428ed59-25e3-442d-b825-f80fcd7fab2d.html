Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 6.6

What to Check For

Ensure that public-facing web applications are reviewed using manual or automated tools or methods at least annually and after any changes. Check that an organization or department that specializes in application security performs the review. Verify that all vulnerabilities found during the review are corrected. Check that the applications are re-evaluated after the corrections.

Why

Reviewing public-facing web applications may help discover and fix vulnerabilities before they are discovered and exploited by attackers.

How to Check

Make sure that the review process is documented. Inspect the review documentation to make sure the reviews are performed using manual or automated tools or methods at least annually or after any changes. Verify that the review is performed by an organization or department that specializes in application security. Review the documentation to make sure that all discovered vulnerabilities are corrected and the applications are re-evaluated after the corrections.

How to Fix

Add a requirement to the information security policy to perform the review using manual or automated tools or methods at least annually or after any changes. Add a requirement to the information security policy to correct all discovered vulnerabilities and re-evaluate applications after the corrections. The review should be performed by an organization or department that specializes in application security. If your company has a department that specializes in application security, it is natural to make them responsible for the review.
