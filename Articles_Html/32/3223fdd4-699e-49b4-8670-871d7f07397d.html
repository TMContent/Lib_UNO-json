Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 8.4

What to Check For

The staff should be familiar with the password policies and procedures.

Why

The staff should be familiar with the password policies and procedures so that they can comply.

How to Check

Interview a sample of the staff to make sure they are familiar with the password policies and procedures. Have the password policy on hand to verify that the staff is familiar with it.

How to Fix

Distribute a copy of the password policy and a memo about the password procedures among the employees and instruct the employees to become familiar with them.
