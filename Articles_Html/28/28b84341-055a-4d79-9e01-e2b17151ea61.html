Applies To

  • PCI DSS Requirement 3.4

Policy Statement

Protect stored PANs.

Policy Purpose

The PAN should be rendered unreadable whenever it is stored to prevent disclosure in the event of a successful compromise. Encrypt the cardholder data on removable media to make sure that, if the media falls into the possession of an attacker, the cardholder data will remain secure.

Related Guidance

  • Store Passwords Securely - Treat PANs like passwords.
