Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 5.1.1

What to Check For

All installed anti-virus programs should detect, remove, and protect against all known types of malware.

How to Check

Refer to the vendor's web site, documentation or tech support to verify that your anti-virus can detect, remove, and protect against viruses, trojans, worms, spyware, adware, and rootkits.

How to Fix

If your anti-virus does not adequately handle all known types of malware threats, install and configure an anti-virus that does. In some cases, updating the anti-virus to the latest version may be sufficient.
