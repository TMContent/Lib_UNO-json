Applies to

  • PCI DSS Requirement 12.7

What to Check For

Background checks should be performed before hiring new employees who will have access to cardholder data or the cardholder data environment.

Why

Background checks ensure that the potential employee is not a known criminal and is representing themselves truthfully.

How to Check

Interview the Human Resources staff to verify that background checks are performed before hiring new employees who will have access to cardholder data or the cardholder data environment.

How to Fix

Choose an agency that can perform background checks. Attorneys and private investigators can often provide this service for a small fee. Instruct Human Resources personnel to conduct background checks before hiring new employees who will have access to cardholder data or the cardholder data environment.
