Applies To

  • PCI DSS Requirement 8.2.1

Policy Statement

Protect stored and transmitted authentication credentials.

Policy Purpose

Encrypting passwords during storage and transmission ensures that an attacker cannot use them even if he gains access to them.

Related Guidance

  • Store Passwords Securely
  • Protect Authentication Credentials in Transit

Related Checklist Items

  • Passwords Are Stored Securely
  • Authentication Credentials in Transit Are Protected
