Applies to

  • PHP

What to Do

Configure PHP to make sessions time-out after a certain period of inactivity.

Why

Limiting session life-time makes it harder to hijack sessions.

How

To make sessions time-out after a certain period of inactivity, edit the php.ini file to include the following directive:

session.gc_maxlifetime = 600

The snippet above sets the idle time-out period to 600 seconds.
